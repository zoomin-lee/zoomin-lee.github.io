I am a Ph.D. candidate in Scalable Graphics, Vision, and Robotics (SGVR) Lab at KAIST advised by Prof. Sung-Eui Yoon. I received my M.S. degree in Artificial Intelligence from KAIST and received my B.S. degree in Artificial Intelligence and Mechanical Engineering from SungKyunKwan University in 2022. My research interests are in 3D vision and computer vision. I am interested in generating 3D scenes with diffusion models as world models for autonomous driving, etc. My future research includes generating 3D scenes with temporal consistency.
